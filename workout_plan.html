Warm-up rulePerform a full general warm-up and exercise-specific warm-up every workout as outlined below (should only take 5-10 mins max).
Week 1 intro/deloadFor the first week of the program, most sets are taken to an RPE of ~7-9. This means leaving 1-3 reps in the tank on most exercises. After the first week, intensity increases and the last set of every exercise is taken to failure.
Early Sets / Last SetsAll working sets are broken up into Early Sets and Last Sets. Early Sets come before the Last Set and are usually slightly easier. The Last Set is pushed to failure on all exercises besides intro/deload weeks.
Exercise demosEach exercise has a clickable link which demonstrates how to perform the exercise. Watch each exercise demo before starting the workout.
